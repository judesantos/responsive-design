Anticipated to be complete in Summer of 2019, Newhall Crossings plans to be a catalyst for the up and coming area of Old Town Newhall in Santa Clarita, CA. The new development will offer a total of 20,076 square feet of available retail space allowing tenants to choose anywhere from 1,000 Sf to 10,000 Sf per suite.

Retailers will benefit from the adjacent 372 stall public parking structure, central courtyard, and neighboring Laemmle Art-House Movie Theater, attracting over 250,000 customers annually. The tree lined street provides a walkable area attracting residents and newcomers alike from all over Santa Clarita with Newhall Crossings as its northern anchor of the premier pedestrian orientated commercial corridor.

The outdoor seating along Main Street and within the Courtyard will allow restaurants to host guests in an open-air environment. Retailers also have the ability to take advantage of the many weekly events that happen on Main Street as well as newly planned events in the central Courtyard of Newhall Crossings. For the full list of all events that take place in Old Town Newhall, please visit otna.org
